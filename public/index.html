FAQ

Does the student have to give any entrance examination prior to joining MBBS in Universities of Russia?

Unlike the other countries, there are no pre-qualifying examinations like TOEFL, IELTS, etc. for admission to MBBS course in Russia. The admissions are based on the 12th standard marks for the Graduate courses and as per the equivalency of the Indian graduate degree with the Russian degrees for the postgraduate courses. All admissions are strictly on a first come first serve basis.

What is the Eligibility Criteria to get admission for pursuing MBBS in Russia?

As per Medical Council of India the minimum qualification for Indian Students to study in these countries is 50% in physics, chemistry and biology in 12th standard. In case of SC/ST it is 40%. The minimum age should be 17 years. Indian students intending to obtain primary medical qualification from any medical institution outside India, on or after 1st May 2018, shall have to mandatorily qualify NEET "National Eligibility -cum Entrance Test for Admission to MBBS Course.

What is the duration of MBBS course in Russia?

Duration of MBBS in Russia in English Medium is 6years. Students can also opt for MBBS in Russian Medium. Duration of which is 7 years which includes 1 year as Russian Language Training (Preparatory Faculty).
